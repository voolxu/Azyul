Introduction

Welcome!

 

If you don't know already, this is a profile pack for Honorbuddy!

 

What is Honorbuddy? It's an artificial intelligence program designed to automate the repetitive tasks in World of Warcraft. You'd probably recognize it as a "bot."

 

This profile pack is designed to farm items of interest, transmog sets and grind dungeons.

 

Scroll down to get more information about each farm category. Or navigate using the menu at the top.

Transmog Farms

The oldest in the pack!

 

Purpose

These profiles are designed to farm entire sets of transmog gear.

 

 

Tips

Tier Recolor profiles aren't designed to vendor, mail or repair. However, while running the dungeon they will only loot the boss that drops the item.

 

Subset profiles will vendor, mail and repair. Some Subset profiles will randomize the farming locations to avoid suspicion, locations will randomize between vendor runs.

 

 

Tier Recolors

These are items that are modeled to look like tier set items, however they're recolored! An example of a Tier and it's Recolor would be the Netherwind Regalia, tier 2 for mages.

Profiles in this pack will be tagged with: [HD] [ND] and (RC)

- [HD] is Heroic Difficulty, these items will only be farmed once daily.

- [ND] is Normal Difficulty, these items will be farmed until obtained.

- (RC) is Recolor, this tag tells you that the items farmed will be a recolored version of the set.

 

 

Subsets

These are items that aren't actually sets, but match together. Subsets are usually dropped in the environment and are BoE, so they can be sold for a profit.

An example of a subset would be the Buccaneer's Regalia.

Dungeon Farms

Some dungeon farms utilize the [DD] system!

 

Purpose

These profiles are designed to farm dungeons whether it be for enchanting materials, gold farms, rare item hunting, achievement farming or for reputation farms.

These profiles will kill and loot (almost) every mob in the dungeon, and they're usually slow paced.

 

 

Tips

These profiles have a mail, vendor, and repair routine. This routine will activate when the user has less than 10 bagslots after finishing an instance. The bot will not mail if you do not have a "Mail To" set in Honorbuddy's settings.

 

 

What's [DD]?

[DD] is the Detection Diamond.

Prior to entering the instance, the bot will pause and ask you how you want the instance ran.

Some options are: Heroic Mode, Regular Mode, Lv.90 Gold Farm, Lv.85 Gold Farm.

Each mode will be optimized for whichever option you pick.

(ex: The Lv.90 gold farm will pull huge packs of mobs whereas the Lv.85 gold farm will pull smaller packs.)

Item Farming

Mounts, Rares, Gold and more!

 

Purpose

Profiles in this pack are dedicated to farm rare mobs, pets, mounts, gold, reputation and basically anything else - until stopped by the user!

 

 

Tips

Sometimes profiles in this pack will farm transmog items, but do not confuse this with the other transmog profiles. Anytime there's a profile in this category that farms a transmog, it will only be farming one specific item.

The transmog farming category is for entire armor sets.

These profiles will vendor, mail and repair.

 

 

Extra

These profiles may be risky to use, they behave a lot like a typical 'grind' bot which simply means it will repetitively kill and farm the exact same mobs over and over until stopped by the user.

Please limit your farm time while using these profiles!

 

Need-To-Know!

You need to know this.

- All profiles in this pack operate on the 'Questing' botbase!

- Some profiles may require you to be in a specific location. You may want to start the bot in Stormwind or Orgrimmar!

- Some profiles in this pack need behaviors, however the behaviors are still being re-coded and currently do not work.

- Not every profile will vendor / mail / repair!

- These profiles have dated code, so some things may not work properly! I'm in the process of fixing them.
